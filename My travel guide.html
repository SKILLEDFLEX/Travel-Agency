Basic Guidelines

Follow these essential travel tips to ensure a smooth journey.

  • Arrive at the airport 3 hours before international flights
  • Keep digital and physical copies of your documents
  • Check visa requirements for your destination

Cultural Etiquette Tips For Each Continent

  • Africa: Greet elders with respect, avoid using your left hand when giving/receiving items in some regions.
  • Asia: Remove shoes before entering homes; avoid pointing feet at people/statues.
  • Europe: Be punctual; tipping is appreciated but varies (check local norms).
  • North America: Respect personal space; tipping is expected (15–20%).
  • South America: Greetings are often warm (cheek kisses, handshakes); don’t rush conversations.
  • Oceania: Be casual but polite; respect Indigenous cultures and sites.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa for Europe?

It depends on your nationality. South African passport holders generally require a Schengen visa.

What items are prohibited in hand luggage?

No sharp objects, large liquids (over 100ml), or flammable items.

Can I travel with just a digital boarding pass?

Yes, most airlines accept digital passes, but keep a screenshot just in case.
